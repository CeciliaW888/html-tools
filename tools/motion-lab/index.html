What this teaches

Staggered reveal

Use offset timing between headline lines and support elements so the eye has a controlled reading path instead of receiving everything at once.

Layered depth

Parallax works when each layer moves at a different amplitude and purpose. Background drift should be quieter than foreground motion.

Glow discipline

Glow is strongest as punctuation. Raise or lower intensity to see how fast cinematic becomes gaudy when too many edges emit light.

Pacing and hold

The quote scene shows that motion design is also about when nothing happens. A clean hold lets the language carry weight.
