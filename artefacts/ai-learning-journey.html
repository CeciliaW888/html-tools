What I Learned Along the Way

  • Build in public. Sharing = learning faster. You get feedback, accountability, and momentum. It doesn't have to be polished.
  • Play the long game. AI tools change every week. The meta-skill is learning how to learn. Small experiments compound over time.
  • Start small, think big. You don't need to dedicate weekends. 30 minutes a week. Try one thing. See what happens.
